Give your application
the attention it deserves

As the browser becomes home to more and more application logic, it requires the expertise of a skilled front-end developer. I work with you to ensure the front end of your application gets the attention it deserves, developing extensible, scaleable, high-performance solutions that serve as the glue between the server and the browser. By identifying and leveraging the similarities among front-end design and application components, I create an architecture that facilitates the rapid development of new features and emphasizes reusability from the start.

My clients run the gamut from startups to interactive agencies to teams working on mature web applications. Though my domain is the browser, I speak fluently with server-side developers, and work closely with them to integrate a comprehensive front-end solution into your application as a whole.

More than slicing and dicing

Quality front-end development goes far beyond reproducing pretty Photoshop files — it encompasses thoughtful templating systems that promote rapid development by integrating with server-side code and eliminating repetition; JavaScript that’s written to behave like an application, not a slew of unrelated and difficult-to-maintain behaviors; semantic HTML and cross-browser-compatible CSS that promotes consistency across a site; and best practices that increase page performance and enhance the user experience.

A simple value proposition

I bring a sophisticated, holistic point of view to your unique front-end challenges, helping you create rich, high-performance interfaces and applications. My solutions emphasize rapid development, reuse, consitency, and cross-browser compatibility. I deliver a comprehensive front-end solution that takes into account the wide range of questions involved in developing a robust client-side application — and save you the expense of having to solve front-end problems on your own.
